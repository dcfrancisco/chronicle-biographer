# Vision

**Product name:** Chronicle

**Tagline:** A conversation with your life.

Chronicle helps people make sense of their past by turning scattered documents, conversations, and media into a living, curated life story. The goal is to surface recurring themes, unanswered questions, and evidence-backed narratives that a user can refine into books, essays, or personal journals.

Principles:
- Human-centered: make memory discovery intimate and private by default.
- Evidence-first: every claim links back to source artifacts.
- Iterative: support hypothesis → investigation → theme promotion.
- Composable: memories, artifacts, and narratives interoperate across features.
