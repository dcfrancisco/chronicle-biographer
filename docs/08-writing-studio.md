# Writing Studio

The Writing Studio is the user's creative surface for assembling `Theme`s into `Chapters` and `BookDraft`s. Provide ergonomic editing, source citation insertion, and export options (PDF, EPUB).

Features:
- Draft editor with inline citations to `Artifact`s and `Observation`s
- Theme-to-chapter scaffolding and drag/drop ordering
- Versioning and draft history
- Export presets for self-publishing or print
