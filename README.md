# Chronicle

## A conversation with your life.

Most software helps you remember.

Chronicle helps you understand.

Chronicle is an AI Biographer — a long-term witness that helps people discover patterns, contradictions, lessons, and meaning from a lifetime of memories, stories, documents, conversations, and experiences.

It does not simply collect memories.

It investigates them.

It asks questions.

It challenges assumptions.

It looks for recurring themes.

It helps transform fragments into a story.

---

## Why Chronicle Exists

Most people leave behind thousands of pages of material:

* journals
* photos
* emails
* documents
* projects
* conversations
* social media posts

Yet very little of it becomes understanding.

Chronicle was created to answer questions that traditional note-taking systems cannot:

* Who am I?
* How did I become this person?
* What patterns define my life?
* What beliefs have changed?
* What lessons have I learned?
* What should survive after I'm gone?

---

## The Idea

Imagine spending years talking to a biographer.

Someone who remembers everything.

Someone who notices contradictions.

Someone who asks better questions.

Someone who gradually builds an understanding of your life.

Chronicle attempts to become that biographer.

---

## Example

You write:

> I taught myself programming because nobody taught me.

Chronicle may respond:

> Observation:
>
> Independent learning appears repeatedly throughout your life story.
>
> Confidence: 82%

Then ask:

> Who taught you persistence?

Years later, Chronicle may discover:

> Theme:
>
> Self-Reliance
>
> Evidence:
>
> 47 memories
> 12 interviews
> 8 major life decisions

That theme becomes part of your story.
